Frequently Asked Questions

How does the service work?

We come to your property on your scheduled day, thoroughly clean your yard of all pet waste, and dispose of it properly. You don't even need to be home!

Do I need to sign a contract?

Nope! All our services are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

What if it rains or snows?

We work in most weather conditions. If severe weather prevents us from servicing your yard, we'll reschedule for the next available day at no extra charge.

Is the initial cleanup really 50% off?

Yes! With your first month of recurring service, we discount the inital clean at 50% the value!.

What if I do not need a inital cleanup?
